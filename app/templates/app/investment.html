INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT

At PineHill we play an important role in promoting investment opportunities through expert financial engineering. We provide a comprehensive range of investment products & services including portfolio management and advisory, among others. We personally cater to the clients’ requirements on a one-on-one basis to provide financial solutions that best suit their investment needs. The team brings together highly skilled risk and portfolio managers as well as research analysts, who have a proven track record in both emerging and developed capital markets who work round the clock to offer you:

INVESTMENT STRATEGY

We work with you to capture your goals, risk tolerance, return needs, and time horizon in an Investment Policy Statement that guides further investing. Our compliance program and benchmarks evaluation; selection of and due-diligence on investment managers and vendors; and a variety of proprietary portfolio analytics make sure the investment strategy continues to meet your original or adjusted objectives.

INVESTMENT PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT

One of our most important roles is to assist our clients in determining a suitable and appropriate asset allocation strategy by considering investment time horizon, risk tolerance, objectives and cash flow needs. We invest for the long term by establishing appropriate asset allocations, however; we proactively adjust those allocations and strategies depending upon changing market conditions.

PRINCIPAL FINANCE

We work with associate consultants and partner businesses to offer flexible primary financing solutions and engage in secondary market investing, across the capital structure. Our team has experience across a variety of industry groups including real estate, fintech, infrastructure, technology, leisure and healthcare.
